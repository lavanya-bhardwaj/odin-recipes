Description

Pizza is a classic Italian dish made with dough, sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings.

It is one of the most popular comfort foods in the world and can be customized in countless ways.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Spread sauce over the dough.
  3. Add cheese and toppings.
  4. Bake until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot.
